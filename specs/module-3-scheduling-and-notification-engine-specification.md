# Module 3: Scheduling and Notification Engine

## 1\. Objective

To automate the tracking of maintenance intervals and ensure timely reminders are delivered via Google Calendar and Email.

## 2\. Notification Channels

* **Google Calendar:** Automatic injection of recurring maintenance events.  
* **Email Alerts:** Structured summaries sent to the user's primary inbox.

## 3\. Automated Content Requirements

Each notification/reminder should contain the following metadata to facilitate immediate action:

* **Specific Device:** The name and category of the item.  
* **Task Description:** Clear instructions on the required maintenance.  
* **Required Parts:** Specific part numbers and model IDs.  
* **Shopping Links:** Curated links to verified vendors for replacement parts.  
* **Tutorial Resources:** A relevant YouTube link demonstrating the specific maintenance task.

## 4\. Logical Intervals

* **Static Schedules:** Based on manufacturer recommendations (e.g., "Every 6 months" for water filters).  
* **Seasonal Tasks:** Linked to time of year (e.g., "Spring cleaning" for outdoor units).
